About Lingfield United Trust Charity

Aims and Objectives

To relieve the needs of poor persons of good character who are aged or infirm and have resided in the parish of Lingfield, Surrey including Dormansland and parts of Baldwins Hill, in particular but not exclusively through:

  • the provision and maintenance of almshouse accommodation
  • the provision of grants.
